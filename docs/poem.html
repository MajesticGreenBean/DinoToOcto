I tried my best, don't laugh

In modern times, where life meaning fades,
A tale of love unfolds in strangest of pace,
Two unexpected souls, in myriad shades,
Find solace amidst boring space.

A dinosaur, scaly beast of bygone age,
Walking shell, with nothing more to chase,
Yet deep within, in his fossilized cage,
A longing wish, to be blessed with love's grace.

An octopus, with arms that always explore,
A colorful life, goals and deep secrets to tell,
Her ink-black eyes, gazes up from ocean floors,
Dreaming of a love she has never felt.

Amidst the chaos of life's frantic race,
Two hearts skipped a beat, for the first time this way,
Against odds and trials, hand in hand they keep,
Come! All struggles, together we face.

Though dinosaurs and ocotpi may seem,
The most illogical pairing in life's grand scheme,
Through each new lifetime, they unite and reach,
A happy ending, again and again.
